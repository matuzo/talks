* HTTP/1.1 more than 15 years old
* Based on Googles SPDY
* Faster connection speeds 
* One connection for parallelism (no more sprites, concatenation, etc.)
* HTTPS not necessarily mandatory (but for Firefox and Chrome it will be)
* Read more in the [HTTP/2 FAQ](https://http2.github.io/faq/)
